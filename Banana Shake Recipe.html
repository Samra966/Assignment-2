Banana Shake Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vanilla ice cream
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Peel the bananas and cut them into chunks.
  2. In a blender, combine the banana chunks, milk, vanilla ice cream, honey (optional), and a few ice cubes.
  3. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour the banana shake into glasses and serve chilled.
